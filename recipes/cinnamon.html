Cinnamon rolls

Cinnamon rolls. As the name suggests, it's made with cinnamon sugar and bread flour.

Ingredients.

  • Cinnamon powder
  • Bread flour
  • Eggs
  • Brown sugar
  • Butter
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix the flour, butter, salt and eggs
  2. Allow dough to rise
  3. Put sugar and cinnamon powder
  4. Cock in the over
